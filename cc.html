✺ Celebrating diversity in art, music, and fashion

background —

Cultural Celebration is an annual event coordinated by Marshall College at UC San Diego that celebrates diversity in cultures around us. This year, our theme focused on art, music, and fashion from around the world. As part of the college’s design team, I closely collaborated with another graphic designer to create the visual identity, print and digital assets, and merchandise for the event.
